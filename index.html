About Me

I'm Aditya, an entrepreneur passionate about transforming the fitness and sports coaching industry. With a background in Mathematics and Computing from IIT Delhi, I've founded multiple successful startups that combine technical innovation with practical fitness solutions.

My journey began with Nutrition Buddy, evolved through Coachbase, and culminated in Sports by Coachbase - each venture addressing unique challenges in the fitness ecosystem and delivering measurable results for customers.

I specialize in building sustainable business models, developing standardized coaching frameworks, and creating engaging community experiences that drive retention and growth. My approach combines data-driven decision making with a deep understanding of customer needs in the fitness and sports sectors.
